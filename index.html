Top three places to visit in Lagos

Visit the Lekki Conservation Center

New Oshodi Bus Terminal to enjoy the bus ride into the city

The Oshodi transport interchange is not just a bus terminal. It has become the newest tourist destination in Lagos. It is a multi three-story facility featuring three separate terminals linked by the skywalk bridge.
